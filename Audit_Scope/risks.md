# Risk Analysis – SauceDemo

This document outlines key functional and usability risks identified during the initial
analysis of the SauceDemo e-commerce application. The risks are based on observed user
experience issues and their potential impact on business-relevant user flows.

---

## R1 – Authentication model is not clear for first-time users

Users may not understand the authentication model of the application due to the lack of
clear information that only predefined test accounts are supported.

**Potential impact:**
- User confusion during first interaction
- Inability to access the application
- Early abandonment of the product

**Priority:** High
---

## R2 – Lack of feedback after using "Reset App State"

The application does not provide clear feedback after using the "Reset App State" option,
which may cause users to be uncertain whether the action was successful and what data was
affected.

**Potential impact:**
- Confusion and repeated actions
- Accidental data reset
- Reduced trust in system behavior

**Priority:** Medium

---

## R3 – Lack of product reviews or ratings

Products do not contain reviews or ratings, which may reduce user trust and make it
difficult for users to evaluate products before adding them to the cart.

**Potential impact:**
- Reduced confidence in product selection
- Lower user engagement
- Abandoned purchase decisions

**Priority:** Medium
